Your Mining Data Specialist

Hi, I'm a professional mining engineer who discovered the power of data visualization to transform complex mining operations into actionable insights. After years of working in the field, I realized that many mining companies weren't leveraging their operational data to its full potential.

That's why I founded Project Data Consultants - to bring my dual expertise in mining engineering and data analytics to help companies like yours make smarter decisions.
